What is Human Design?

Human Design is a sophisticated system that integrates principles from astrology, the I Ching, Kabbalah, the Hindu-Brahmin chakra system, and quantum mechanics to create a personalized energetic blueprint. It reveals an individual's innate strengths, decision-making strategies, and life purpose, offering a framework to align personal and professional endeavors with natural tendencies. Developed by Ra Uru Hu in 1987, this system provides insights into how one interacts with the world, processes information, and thrives in specific environments. For professionals, understanding Human Design can enhance self-awareness, improve team dynamics, and optimize leadership styles—making it a valuable tool in job interviews and career development.
