The Vision

Building the marketplace that's transforming how businesses staff and how professionals work.

Shift Spotter connects vetted, credential-verified shift workers with businesses that need reliable coverage—without the overhead of agencies or the headaches of no-shows. Workers set their own schedules and get paid instantly. Businesses access qualified talent in minutes with transparent, hours-worked-only pricing.

Leading product strategy, platform architecture, and go-to-market execution. Leveraging 20+ years of SaaS leadership—including scaling platforms to 150K+ concurrent users and building CI/CD pipelines that cut release cycles and reduced churn by 60%—to create workforce infrastructure that works for both sides of the equation.

We're solving the $500B+ staffing industry's biggest friction points: unpredictable coverage for businesses, inflexible schedules for workers, and opaque pricing for everyone.
