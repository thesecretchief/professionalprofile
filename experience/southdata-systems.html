Acquisition Transition

Key Asset Retained Through Acquisition

I came to SouthData as part of the Micro Images acquisition. The systems and knowledge I brought were critical enough that I was retained as a key asset to help integrate operations and drive the next phase of growth.

The Transformation

SouthData wasn't just one company—we were acquiring and integrating multiple organizations. I was instrumental in the regional acquisitions, bringing the operational excellence I'd developed at Micro Images to new teams and facilities. But the real transformation came from what we did next.

I led a complete operational overhaul based on extensive time-and-motion studies. We analyzed every process, measured every workflow, and identified every bottleneck. The result? A revolutionary on-demand staffing model that allowed us to flexibly scale our core business processes while maintaining quality and compliance.

I replaced and managed all on-site equipment and systems, but my work extended far beyond our walls. I worked heavily with clients across medical management, ERP, HR, and materials management systems—integrating our services into their platforms and helping leadership across multiple industries move from paper to electronic records.
