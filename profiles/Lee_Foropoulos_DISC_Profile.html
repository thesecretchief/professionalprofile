What is the DISC Assessment?

The DISC assessment is a behavioral analysis tool developed by William Moulton Marston, categorizing individuals into four primary personality traits: Dominance, Influence, Steadiness, and Conscientiousness. It provides insights into how I interact with others, approach tasks, and handle challenges.
